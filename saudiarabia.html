Saudi Arabia and Circular Crops

In Saudi Arabia, created center pivot irrigation to grow their circular crops. They are many irrigation projects scattered throughtout world. Nonrenewable fossil water is mined from depths as great as 1 km (3,000 ft), pumped to the surface, and distributed via large center pivot irrigation feeds. The circular fields range from a few hundred meters to as much as 3km (1.9 miles), and contain crops such as alfafa to wheat. Decades before, most was pure desert with rainfall and water so rare, in towns to be able to survive.
